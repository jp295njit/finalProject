Neer Dosa

Neer dosa is a type of Konkani crepe-like dish that is eaten for breakfast in many Konkani households. Though it can be found throughout South India, it is a staple on rainy mornings.

Prawns Gassi

Most Konkani-speaking communities live by the coast of Western India, so dishes highlighting seafood are not uncommon. Prawns gassi is an unmistakably spicy, but delicious curry!

Sanna Polo

Sanna polo, also known as cabbage dosa/crepe, is a savory dish eaten along with a main meal. It can be made with seafood as well. The signature ingredient is grated cocount.
